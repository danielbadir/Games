Mini Motor
Maze

Unity 6 · Android → iOS · ¾ Isometric

A charming top-down racer where you navigate hand-crafted mazes, collect gems, and use car-specific abilities. The first car — Sunny Coupe — is a lightweight plastic speedster with a turbo boost. Designed around a 3-star level loop with escalating difficulty across 20 Forest Trails levels.

✓ ¾ isometric camera with smooth follow
✓ Car abilities system (AbilityBase architecture)
✓ Star rating per level (move-efficiency formula)
✓ Level complete screen with retry & next-level
✓ All EditMode tests written (20 tests passing)
✓ Analytics events wired (no Debug.Log-only paths)

Galaxy
Diamond

Unity 6 · Android → iOS · Match-3

A match-3 puzzle game with an AI Director called IRIS that watches your play and responds — placing cursed gems, boosting blessed ones, and shifting the sky background to reflect your momentum. Features procedural music, special gem types, chain scoring, and a full meta-progression system.

✓ IRIS AI Director — 4 states (Idle / In Flow / Frustrated / Acting)
✓ 4 special gem types (ResonanceCore, ResonanceBomb, LineBurst, ColorNova)
✓ Procedural background music (C major pentatonic, 116 BPM)
✓ Chain multiplier scoring (×1–×4+) with escalating colors
✓ Crystal quota level-complete mechanic
✓ Full 8×8 board: swap, match, cascade, fill, animations
