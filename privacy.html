Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-06-18 Owner: LPagesAppLabs / D.Badi Contact: contact@lpagesapplabs.com

The short version: The games.lpagesapplabs.com marketing site collects no personal data. Our games are in development. When they launch, each app's in-app privacy policy will describe exactly what data that app collects. This document covers the current state.

Age notice: LPagesAppLabs games are designed for a general audience and are not specifically directed at children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information in connection with any of our services, please contact us at contact@lpagesapplabs.com and we will address it promptly.

01 Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the games.lpagesapplabs.com marketing website and, when they become available, the mobile apps Mini Motor Maze and Galaxy Diamond. As of the effective date, neither app has been released to the public.

02 Marketing Website Data

The games.lpagesapplabs.com marketing website:

  • Sets no cookies
  • Runs no analytics scripts
  • Stores no user data on our servers
  • Requires no account creation

If you use the "Notify Me" form, your browser opens your email client locally. No data is submitted to our servers — the email originates from your device. We receive only what you choose to send.

03 Apps: Data Collection (Pre-Launch)

Mini Motor Maze and Galaxy Diamond are in active development. Their final data collection practices will be determined by their architecture and publishing requirements. Before launch, we will publish a complete, in-app privacy policy. The following are current intentions:

  • Gameplay data: local device only — progress, scores, and settings stored on-device
  • Crash reports: if enabled, anonymous crash data may be sent to a third-party service (will be disclosed and opt-out offered)
  • Analytics: if used, will be disclosed with opt-out mechanism at first launch
  • Advertising: if ads are implemented, this policy will be updated with full advertiser disclosure before any ad SDK is added
  • Accounts: no account system planned at this time

We are committed to minimal data collection, clear disclosure before launch, and COPPA/GDPR compliance in every published build.

04 Third-Party Services (Website)

The marketing website loads fonts from Google Fonts (fonts.bunny.net). This is a standard HTTP request; Google's servers receive your IP address as part of normal web operation. We do not use advertising networks, tracking pixels, or analytics on this website.

05 Children's Privacy (COPPA / GDPR-Kids)

Our games are designed for a general audience. They are not specifically directed at children under 13. Age ratings appropriate to each game's content will be applied through the relevant app store before any public release. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (US — COPPA) or under 16 (EU — GDPR Article 8). If child-directed content or features are added to any app, we will implement age verification and parental consent mechanisms prior to that version's launch, in full compliance with applicable law.

If you believe a child has submitted personal information without appropriate consent, contact us immediately at contact@lpagesapplabs.com and we will delete it.

06 Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR (for EEA residents), you have the following rights with respect to any personal data we hold about you:

Access
Request a copy of your data.
Rectification
Correct inaccurate data.
Erasure
Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten").
Portability
Receive your data in a portable format.
Objection
Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
Restriction
Request that we restrict processing while a dispute is resolved.

To exercise any right, email contact@lpagesapplabs.com. We respond within 30 days.

07 International Transfers

We are an independent developer based in the EU. We do not transfer personal data to countries outside the EEA except where a third-party service (e.g. crash reporting, app store distribution) requires it, in which case we rely on the European Commission's standard contractual clauses or the third party's adequacy decision.

08 Data Retention

Email correspondence is retained until you request deletion. If we launch analytics or crash reporting, we will specify retention periods in the in-app privacy policy at launch.

09 Changes to This Policy

We will update this policy materially before each app launch to reflect actual data practices. The effective date above will be updated. Continued use of our services after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10 Contact & Data Controller

Data Controller: LPagesAppLabs / D.Badi

Email: contact@lpagesapplabs.com

For GDPR-related complaints, you also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority.
